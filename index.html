Redesigned GlassVU Redesigned ReportVU Redesigned Video

<< Redesigned GlassVU >>

The following are the changes we've made: we have coloured the menu as darker blue to give a clear contrast from the content we have kept the logo on the top to indicate its branding we have added a small mark to indicate the designated location of the user we have moved the setting button on the top-right, next to the designated location we have added the description of the page for each menu to explain what the user can do on each page we have distinguished each column to ensure smooth navigation we have added a QR code to make sure that the devices are well-seen and used


<< Redesigned ReportVU >>

The following are the changes we've made: we have applied the similar UI setting on the menu and on the top of the portal we have applied the similar UI setting in terms of the menu description we have changed the graph about the usage from the linear graph to a segmented graph depending on use we have differentiated the history (underneath the graph) by adding more detail such as the identification of caller, the identification of participants, the duration of time for each calls, status of recording, and indication where the saved files can be found


<< Redesigned Video-Conference session window >>

The following are the changes we've made: we have changed how the navigation bar appears to be: the navigation icon bar for the Desktop PC or Mac will appear at the bottom of the screen (following the industry convention) and the navigation icon bar to control the smart-glasses will appear in the left side of the centred display box. Basically, if the mouse does not hover around the biggest screen in this video-session, the separated buttons for the smart glasses will not appear. we have grouped the icons together and distributed them to either the left, the centre, and the right depending on its purpose we have utilized the blank white space as much as we can by re-sizing the participant screens.

Context & Background

TeleVU Innovations Inc. is a privately-owned telecommunication corporation that connects the clinicians and the patients on a remote-basis to reduce the wait times and to offer improved medical assistance.

TeleVU Innovations Inc. offers two different products that are connected to each other. One of them is the access to the desktop website that clinicians can use to treat patients remotely.

On Desktop website, the customers can sign-up, cancel, call another customer who use the same program, initiate a video-call, record a video-call (while in session) or review the recorded videos in history.


TeleVU Website Portal

The other product is the service in glasses which is wearable. This machine is usually provided to the guardians or nurses who can assist the patients.
Similar to the Desktop website, the patient's guardian(s) or the nurse-in-charge can either dial or accept the call from clinicians who are calling them from the Desktop website.
