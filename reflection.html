Reflection

Looking back to this project, I am thankful that we had a gentle client.
Still, we had a few challenges that fought us:

Research

It was not easy to recruit participants for this case study as the majority were occupied with patients.
The team ended up also interviewing the medical interns who were also running in the field.

Teamwork

Since we were all working asynchronously, we heavily relied on tools such as Figma, Google Spreadsheet, Google Excel, and Miro altogether to keep up with the dates.

Workload

The amount of workload was overwhelming, but we were very happy to see that a. the redesign worked out very well, b. my teammates and the client have understood the importance of the research, and c. I have learned some valuable designing tools such as Figma.

For the Next Step

If there is anything that I wish that things were better:

  • I still feel that it was unfortunate for us to miss the chance to learn further regarding the client company's business structure.
  • I still hope that he allows some tiny changes such as modifying the titles for videos that are saved to the history section after the users are finished with recording the session for the convenience of the users, if the client chooses to work with another team of designers in the next opportunity.
